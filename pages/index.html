The ActiveCampaign Sync application allows for automatic synchronization of information between Google Sheets™ and the ActiveCampaign™ platform. With this tool, it's possible to map Google Sheets™ fields with custom fields in ActiveCampaign™, allowing information to be updated in real-time.

To use the application, it's necessary to configure the URL and API token of ActiveCampaign™, as well as selecting the leads list and email column in Google Sheets™. Then, it's possible to map add-on columns with custom fields in ActiveCampaign™ such as utm_campaign, utm_source, utm_medium, utm_content, and utm_term.

The application also offers connection testing functionality with ActiveCampaign™ and the ability to view available leads lists and custom fields in the platform.

Additionally, the application includes a customized menu in Google Sheets™ and can be easily configured and personalized by the user. Automatic synchronization is performed through a trigger that is activated whenever a new form is submitted to Google Sheets™.

With ActiveCampaign Sync, it's possible to automate marketing and sales processes, saving time and increasing team productivity.
